# Scaling Laws (2020–2021)

## The Story

175 billion parameters. GPT-3 shows few-shot learning without fine-tuning. OpenAI proves that scale alone creates emergent abilities. The Chinchilla paper later reveals optimal compute-to-data ratios.

## Key Concepts

- **Few-shot learning** — Examples in the prompt, no training needed
- **Scaling laws** — Predictable relationship between size and performance
- **Emergent abilities** — Capabilities that appear only at scale
- **Prompt engineering** — The art of asking the right question

## What You Build

- Explore prompt design with API access
- Compare zero-shot, few-shot, and chain-of-thought prompting

## Key Insight

At sufficient scale, models develop abilities they were never explicitly trained for. Nobody fully understands why.

# The ML Timeline

## Act 1 - The Origin Story
- **1943** McCulloch & Pitts: mathematical neuron
- **1950** Turing: "Computing Machinery and Intelligence"
- **1956** Dartmouth Conference: "AI" coined
- **1957** Rosenblatt: the Perceptron
- **1969** Minsky & Papert: XOR limitation
- **1970s** First AI winter
- **1986** Rumelhart, Hinton & Williams: backpropagation

## Act 2 - The Math Age
- **1995** Vapnik: Support Vector Machines
- **1997** Ensemble methods gain traction
- **1998** LeCun: LeNet-5

## Act 3 - Deep Learning Revolution
- **2009** ImageNet dataset created
- **2012** AlexNet wins ImageNet
- **2014** GANs, GRU, Seq2Seq
- **2015** ResNet, Batch Normalization

## Act 4 - The Transformer Era
- **2017** "Attention Is All You Need"
- **2018** BERT, GPT-1
- **2019** GPT-2
- **2020** GPT-3, Scaling laws

## Act 5 - The Generative Explosion
- **2021** DALL-E
- **2022** Stable Diffusion, ChatGPT
- **2023** GPT-4, LLaMA, open-source race

## Act 6 - The Intelligence Race
- **2024** o1 reasoning, agentic AI
- **2025** Small efficient models, video generation
- **2026** Multimodal + edge AI frontier

# Multimodal Generation (2025–2026)

## The Story

AI generates video with temporal consistency. Models process text, images, audio, and video simultaneously. Multimodal understanding becomes the standard. The frontier is no longer about any single modality — it's about all of them at once.

## Key Concepts

- **Vision-language models** — See and speak
- **Multimodal fusion** — Combine information across modalities
- **Video generation** — Temporal coherence and physics understanding
- **Audio synthesis** — Voice, music, and sound effects from text

## What You Build

- Vision-language pipeline that reasons across images and text
- The frontier of AI today

## Run

```bash
cd multimodal
python multimodal.py
```

## Key Insight

Human intelligence is multimodal by default. AI had to get there too. The walls between text, vision, and audio are dissolving.
